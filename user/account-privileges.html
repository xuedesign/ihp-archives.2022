會員帳號權限說明

Account privileges explanation for new members

申請會員前須知事項，請先閱讀：Before applying for membership, please read the following important information.

  • 未登錄會員帳號者，僅能搜尋資料庫已開放目錄，瀏覽題名。欲進一步瀏覽詳目訊息，請註冊會員並登錄帳號。Users without a registered account are only able to search the open catalogues of the database and browse the titles. To be able to browse more detailed information, please register and sign in to an account.
  • 本資料庫以個人電子郵件信箱為會員帳號，若密碼遺失，請自行重新設定。This database uses your email address as your account name. If you have forgotten your password, please reset it.
  • 本資料庫會員效期以三年為限，到期後請申請延長或重新申請。A registered account to this database is valid for three years. Once your account has expired, please apply for an extension or reapply for a new account.
  • 若有任何申請會員上的疑慮，請隨時與我們聯繫：For any questions or concerns, please contact us at any time：archives@mail.ihp.sinica.edu.tw

同意上述規範，請按「同意」進入申請會員設定。Agree to the above regulations and press "Agree" to proceed to the next step.
